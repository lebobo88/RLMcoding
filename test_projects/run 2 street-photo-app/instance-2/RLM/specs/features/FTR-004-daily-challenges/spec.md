# FTR-004: Daily Challenge System

**Priority**: P1 (Post-MVP)
**Status**: Approved
**Created**: 2025-12-02

## Overview
Photographer-inspired daily missions that encourage users to practice specific techniques. Challenges rotate daily and are tied to legendary photographers' styles.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] One challenge per day (resets at midnight local time)
- [ ] Challenge card in feed (prominent placement)
- [ ] Difficulty levels (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- [ ] Historical context for each challenge
- [ ] Photo submission interface (optional)
- [ ] AI feedback on submissions
- [ ] Community gallery of submissions
- [ ] Completion tracking and streaks

## Example Challenges
- **"Cartier-Bresson's Decisive Moment"**: Capture perfect timing in 3 shots or less
- **"Maier's Invisible Observer"**: Photograph 5 people who never noticed you
- **"Winogrand's Beautiful Chaos"**: Embrace disorder, shoot 50 frames in 15 minutes
- **"Arbus's Emotional Depth"**: Find and photograph someone showing genuine emotion
- **"Erwitt's Comic Timing"**: Capture irony or humor in everyday life
